# ARIZONA FAST FOOD
## Welcome to Arizona fast food, where your order is what we deliver.

# OVERVIEW
Arizona Fast Food is a program that started 20 years ago and has been globally known. It is led by it's manager: Luisa and has been working very well and has never incured any loses
We are thankful to the management for their work towards the company

# Business Rationale

The purpose of Arizona Fast Food is to:

Provide customers with quick access to fast food services
Improve customer experience through an easy-to-use website
Promote online food ordering and delivery
Increase business visibility online
Enhance communication between the business and customers

This project demonstrates how technology can help businesses improve efficiency and customer satisfaction.
