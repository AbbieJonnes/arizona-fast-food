ARIZONA FAST FOOD is a family-owned business proudly serving the Nairobi community for over 20 years. What started as a small roadside kitchen has grown into one of the most loved fast food spots in the city.

We are passionate about quality food, warm hospitality, and making every customer feel at home. Every meal is prepared fresh with love and the finest ingredients.
